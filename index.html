About Us

Founded in 2022, KFT Bravada began its journey in the automotive sales market and quickly evolved into a trusted international trading partner. In a short time, the company expanded well beyond its original focus and developed strong activity across agricultural machinery and equipment, electrical power equipment, household goods, consumer products, and wholesale supply solutions.

Today, we actively work with clients in more than 15 countries and continue to grow through reliable sourcing, competitive commercial terms, and consistent service quality. Over the course of our operations, we have processed more than 500,000 orders, a result that reflects both the trust of our partners and the discipline of our internal processes.

We believe strong business relationships are built on clarity, speed, and accountability. That is why our team focuses on responsive communication, dependable logistics coordination, and carefully selected offers that meet practical market demands. We do not simply sell products — we help partners secure the right solutions with confidence.

Our long-term goal is to become a leading name in wholesale and international supply by combining flexible trade expertise with a full range of support services in procurement, coordination, and asset management.
